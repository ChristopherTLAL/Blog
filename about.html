About Shijie

Shijie [ʂʅ51 tɕjɛ35] is an MPhil student at the University of Cambridge. I am interested in psycholinguistics, multilingualism, language acquisition, and quantitative research methods. I conduct research about how previous language experiences will influence the later development of foreign languages, with a focus on meta-linguistic awareness, parameter setting, and language-specific individual differences variables. I am also concerned about the empowerment of special language learners and users.

I’m also an artist focusing on social issues, especially the use of language in society. If you are intereted in it, you could follow me at Art Station or Instagram!

I am also conducting Posthumanism research on how AI-assisted painting process possibly deconstruct the concept of human creativity and how individuals assert ownership of the AI-generated arts.
